Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?

Yes! Creator Tools Suite is completely free and open-source under the MIT license. You can use it for personal or commercial projects.

Do you collect my data?

No. Everything runs 100% in your browser. No data is sent to our servers. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I use this offline?

Yes! The only external dependencies are Tailwind CSS and SortableJS via CDN. After first load, you can work offline (the tools use local Canvas and MediaRecorder APIs).

How do I report a bug?

Open an issue on GitHub! Provide details about what went wrong and steps to reproduce it. We appreciate detailed bug reports.
