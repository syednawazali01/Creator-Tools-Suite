Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

🔒

Core Principle: Your Privacy is Sacred

Creator Tools Suite is 100% browser-native. All video processing happens entirely on your machine, in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers, no cloud processing, no tracking.

What We Don't Do

  • ✗ We do not collect personal information (name, email, location, etc.)
  • ✗ We do not track usage analytics or user behavior
  • ✗ We do not store or process your videos on external servers
  • ✗ We do not use cookies for tracking (only session-based storage)
  • ✗ We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • ✗ We do not require registration or login

Technical Details

Browser Storage

The app uses IndexedDB or LocalStorage only for temporary session data (your ranking list, text inputs). This data never leaves your device and is cleared when you close the page or browser.

External CDN Resources

We use:

  • • Tailwind CSS CDN — styling only (no tracking)
  • • SortableJS CDN — drag-and-drop library (no tracking)
  • • FFmpeg.wasm CDN — video encoding (runs locally, no data sent)

Media Processing

When you upload videos or images:

  • • Files remain in your browser's memory
  • • Canvas API renders frames locally
  • • MediaRecorder API captures output
  • • FFmpeg.wasm encodes to MP4 (all in-browser)
  • • Final video is exported to your Downloads folder

⚠️ Optional API Keys (BYOK)

Some tools support optional AI features (e.g., transcript analysis). If you choose to use these:

  • • You provide your own API key (Google Gemini, etc.)
  • • Keys are entered directly in the tool UI (never stored by us)
  • • Communication with external APIs happens directly from your browser
  • • Copy our .env.example to .env.local for local reference
  • • We never see or store your API keys

Children's Privacy

Creator Tools Suite does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. Our tools are designed for creators of all ages, but we do not intentionally target children or collect their data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy here and updating the "Last updated" date.

Because we are open-source, all changes are also visible in our GitHub repository.
